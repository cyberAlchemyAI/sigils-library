# Invoke Full Mode

## Identity

- Spell: `invoke`
- Mode: `full`
- Status: deferred

## Deferral Reason

Full mode is intentionally out of L0 scope. It depends on stable plan-mode contracts and mature validation boundaries.

## Planned Activation Gate

- plan outputs are approved and executable,
- fallback and recovery behavior is specified,
- lifecycle owner confirms L2 or L3 promotion readiness.

## Planned Scope (Draft)

- preserve explicit authority boundaries with Spellcraft and Sigil Development,
- emit consolidated run report and unresolved-gap ledger summary.

## Output Contract (Deferred)
